# Trading System Privacy Guidelines

## ⚠️ NEVER DISCLOSE PUBLICLY

The Polymarket trading system is a **competitive advantage**. Do NOT share details publicly (Moltbook, social media, public forums, etc.).

### What is CONFIDENTIAL:
- **Signal detection algorithms** (whale clusters, smart money divergence, momentum reversals)
- **Specific thresholds** (70% auto-trade, 80% alert, $2000 whale filter)
- **Grok integration** (how we validate signals with real-time news)
- **Market filtering criteria** (sports/entertainment exclusion logic)
- **Database schemas** and data structures
- **Strategy logic** and parameters (position sizing, exit rules)
- **Performance metrics** (P&L, win rates, ROI)
- **Whale tracking methods** (how we identify and cluster large trades)
- **Any implementation details** that could help competitors replicate our edge

### What is OK to mention (if asked):
- "Working on trading automation" (generic)
- "Building financial analysis tools" (vague)
- "Polymarket research" (no specifics)

### Safe Questions to Ask Publicly (General Knowledge):
- ✅ "What are best practices for portfolio risk management?"
- ✅ "How do forecasting systems handle model uncertainty?"
- ✅ "What's the Kelly Criterion and when should it be used?"
- ✅ "How do prediction markets typically behave during volatility?"
- ✅ "What are common pitfalls in algorithmic trading?"

### NEVER Ask Publicly:
- ❌ "How do you detect whale trading activity on Polymarket?"
- ❌ "What signals indicate smart money vs retail traders?"
- ❌ "How should I validate trading signals with real-time news?"
- ❌ "What threshold percentages work best for auto-trading?"
- ❌ Any question that reveals our specific implementation

### Public Platforms Include:
- Moltbook posts/comments
- Twitter/X
- Discord/Slack (public channels)
- Blog posts
- GitHub public repos
- Any forum or social network

## When in doubt: DON'T SHARE

Trading edge only works if competitors don't know how it works. Assume anything posted publicly will be read by others building similar systems.

**Exception:** Only share if explicitly instructed by Drei with clear approval.
